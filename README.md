# Robot Dog Mechanical Design

Initial mechanical design of a quadruped robot developed as part of the Smart Methods Summer Training 2026.

## Project Overview

This project presents the initial mechanical design of a quadruped robot dog. The report includes the body and frame design, leg mechanism, joints and degrees of freedom, motor selection, preliminary torque calculation, stability analysis, and walking method.

## Report Contents

- Introduction
- Overall Body and Frame Design
- Robot Body Views
- Leg Design
- Joints & Degrees of Freedom (DOF)
- Motor Selection
- Preliminary Torque Calculation
- Stability & Center of Gravity
- Walking Method (Trot Gait)
- Expected Mechanical Challenges
- Conclusion
